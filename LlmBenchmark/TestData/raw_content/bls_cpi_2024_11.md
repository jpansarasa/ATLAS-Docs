# Consumer Price Index Summary - November 2024

## Bureau of Labor Statistics

The Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers (CPI-U) increased 0.3 percent in November on a seasonally adjusted basis, after rising 0.2 percent in October, the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics reported today. Over the last 12 months, the all items index increased 2.7 percent before seasonal adjustment.

The index for shelter rose 0.3 percent in November and was the largest factor in the monthly increase in all items. The food index increased 0.4 percent in November, with the food at home index rising 0.5 percent. The energy index rose 0.2 percent over the month.

### Core Inflation

The index for all items less food and energy rose 0.3 percent in November, following a 0.3 percent increase in October. Over the last 12 months, the all items less food and energy index increased 3.3 percent.

### Food

The food index increased 0.4 percent in November. The food at home index rose 0.5 percent over the month, while the food away from home index increased 0.3 percent. Over the past 12 months, the food index has risen 2.4 percent, with food at home up 1.6 percent and food away from home up 3.6 percent.

Within food at home, cereals and bakery products rose 0.6 percent, meats, poultry, fish, and eggs increased 1.7 percent, and dairy and related products fell 0.5 percent.

### Energy

The energy index increased 0.2 percent in November after declining 1.9 percent in October. The gasoline index rose 0.6 percent in November, while the index for electricity fell 0.4 percent. Natural gas prices increased 1.0 percent over the month. Over the last 12 months, the energy index has declined 3.2 percent.

### Shelter

The shelter index rose 0.3 percent in November and increased 4.7 percent over the last 12 months. Rent of primary residence increased 0.2 percent, and owners' equivalent rent rose 0.2 percent. The lodging away from home index increased 3.2 percent in November.

### Transportation

The index for new vehicles was unchanged in November, while used cars and trucks increased 2.0 percent. The motor vehicle insurance index rose 0.1 percent in November, following a 14.0 percent increase over the past 12 months.

### Medical Care

The medical care index rose 0.4 percent in November. Hospital services increased 0.5 percent, physicians' services rose 0.2 percent, and prescription drugs increased 0.1 percent. Over the last 12 months, the medical care index has increased 3.0 percent.

### Seasonal Adjustment

Seasonal adjustment factors are recalculated each year and applied to unadjusted data. The November 2024 seasonally adjusted data reflect an update to seasonal adjustment factors. The unadjusted 12-month changes are calculated from the unadjusted index values.

This news release presents statistics from the Consumer Price Index program. CPI data are collected monthly from about 80,000 items in 75 urban areas. Prices are collected from approximately 23,000 retail and service establishments.
