# Advance Monthly Sales for Retail and Food Services - November 2024

## U.S. Census Bureau

**Release Date:** December 17, 2024
**Data Reference Period:** November 2024

Advance estimates of U.S. retail and food services sales for November 2024, adjusted for seasonal variation and holiday and trading-day differences, but not for price changes, were $724.6 billion, an increase of 0.7 percent from the previous month, and up 3.8 percent above November 2023.

Total sales for the September 2024 through November 2024 period were up 2.9 percent from the same period a year ago.

The September 2024 to October 2024 percent change was revised from up 0.4 percent to up 0.5 percent.

### Retail Trade

Retail trade sales were up 0.9 percent from October 2024 and up 4.2 percent above last year.

Nonstore retailers were up 9.8 percent from November 2023, while food services and drinking places were up 5.4 percent from last year.

### Sales by Kind of Business

Motor vehicle and parts dealers sales were $148.2 billion in November, up 2.6 percent from October 2024.

Building material and garden equipment and supplies dealers were down 0.4 percent from October 2024.

Electronics and appliance stores sales increased 1.6 percent from the prior month.

Food and beverage stores were up 0.2 percent from October 2024.

Furniture and home furnishings stores were down 0.7 percent from the previous month.

Gasoline stations were down 0.1 percent from October 2024, reflecting lower fuel prices.

Clothing and clothing accessories stores were up 0.6 percent from last month.

Sporting goods, hobby, musical instrument, and book stores were up 0.9 percent.

General merchandise stores were up 0.1 percent from October 2024.

Food services and drinking places were up 0.9 percent from the previous month.

Nonstore retailers showed strong growth, up 1.8 percent from October 2024, driven by continued strength in e-commerce.
